Story of my Life

A poem for the wild hearted. Steering forward in large bold moves.
Guided by a young mystery loving heartbeat.
Shoes are worn out by treading on life's mountain trails.
Spreading oxigants through lungs that can not only
expand like wings but really believe flying is possible
flowing underground like blood gives life in the unseen
wake up beauty, don't slumber in a bed that has become too small for your dreams
dancing wide awake under the wild night sky.
Giantkiller's and peace lover
envision a brighter day with an armour of hope in a war where love cannot loose.
